Prosper Loan Data Exploration¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this project I will investigate what features are best in prediction of the borrower interest rate for the loan, borrowers prosper score and credit grade.

I will investigate from simple univariate relationships up to multivariate relationships.

Dataset Overview¶

There are 113937 loans in the dataset with 81 features for each loan, including borrower's annual percentage rate (APR), status, borrowed amount, debt, etc. I kept only 24 features which I will use in exploratory data analysis.

Distribution of Borrower Rate¶

The distribution of the borrower interest rate is close to normal. The maximum interest rate is a little over 0.35%, while the most common interest rate is between 0.15 and 0.2%. .

Distribution of Prosper Score¶

The prosper score ranges from 1-10, with 10 being the best, or lower risk score. Applicable for loans generated after July 2009. The distribution of ProsperScore is roughly normal. Prosper borrowers have on average prosper score 6.

Borrower Rate vs. Credit Grade¶

The median borrower's interest rate decreases with the increase of credit grade. Some bimodality is seen in credit grades HR, E and D. Credit grades B, A and AA are skewed to the right. The higher the credit grade the more data are clustered around the median.

Credit Grade vs Available Bankcard Credit and Bankcard Utilization¶

The higher available bankcard credit, the higher credit score. The lower bankcard utilization gives the higher credit grade. Low bankcard utilization means only the small percentage of a borrower’s total available credit is currently being utilized.
